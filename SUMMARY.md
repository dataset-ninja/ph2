**PH<sup>2</sup>: A Dermoscopic Image Database for Research and Benchmarking** is a dataset for a semantic segmentation task. It is used in the medical industry. 

The dataset consists of 200 images with 200 labeled objects belonging to 1 single class (*lesion*). There are no pre-defined <i>train/val/test</i> splits in the dataset. Also Dataset includes ***histological_diagnosis***, ***clinical_diagnosis***, ***asymmetry***, ***pigment_network***, ***dots/globules***, ***streaks***, ***regression_areas***,***blue-whitish_veil*** and ***colors*** tags.
